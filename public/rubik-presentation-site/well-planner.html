About Us

✻

Well Planner sprung from noticing a need for a one stop platform solution for wedding event planning businesses.

It's a cloud based application that consists of features around planning one or more wedding events simultaneously by one person or by team of people.

And also has features for managing other business needs, things like vendor contacts, customer base, and business invoicing.

In the end, it's really an all in one platform for supporting the majority of the wedding event planner’s businesses.
